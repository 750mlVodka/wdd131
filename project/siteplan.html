Site Plan: Portfolio

Domain: siteplan.html

Site Name

Yamsir's Portfolio

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase my programming knowledge and the projects I've developed. It will also include a contact section for those who wish to contact me with a job opportunity.

Scenarios

  • What projects have I worked on recently and how can I see them?
  • What are my programming skills and what tools do I use?
  • How can I contact you for a possible collaboration?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (#8AD7EE): Header, nav and body
  • Second Color (#FFFFF): Background, and asides
  • Text Color (#061318): Text color
  • Contrast Color (#F78424) Links and hover texts

Typography

  • Nunito Sans: Titles
  • Roboto Monp: Paragraphs
  • Prompt: Links
